Our Story

Born from Passion,
Built on Trust

Calis Adventures was founded with a single vision: to show the world the breathtaking beauty of East Africa in a way that feels personal, authentic, and unforgettable. From the sweeping savannas of the Masai Mara to the snow-capped peaks of Mount Kenya — every journey we craft is tailored to you.

In just two years of operation, our expert guides, deep local knowledge, and nationwide reach across Kenya have made us a trusted adventure partner — with an estimated impact touching over 2,000 lives.
